Information

  • All students are required to register for classes. You must attend your nominated classes, in line with relevant attendance requirements.

    Waiting lists

    If your preferred class is full you may be able to place yourself on a waiting list. Please note that this does not mean you are registered in the class and there is no guarantee of a space becoming available. It is advised that you select another class rather than find yourself allocated to a class automatically when the waiting lists are turned off.

    Full Classes

    If, for exceptional circumstances, you need to be in a class that is full, you can submit a request for help using the Class Registration Enquiry Management (CREM) system. You may need to supply official documentation to support your request.

There are two ways to complete your class registration

  1. Use the 'Generate and Register into clash free classes' button or the 'Register into all single activities' button to perform an automatic registration into classes for all the selected enrolled subjects.
  2. Use the 'Register' or the 'Change' button to perform a manual registration into classes for each of the selected enrolled subjects in the Timetable Grid.

You can print your timetable after you complete your registration.
